Augmented Reality with AR.js

Point your camera at the Hiro marker to see the 3D model.

How It Works

This is a simple example of marker-based AR using AR.js. The blue box will appear on top of the Hiro marker when viewed through your device's camera.

Steps:

  1. Open this webpage on a mobile device or AR-supported browser.
  2. Allow the browser to access your camera.
  3. Point your camera at the Hiro marker.

You can download the Hiro marker from here.
